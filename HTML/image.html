object-fit: cover : Behavior: The image is resized to fill the container while maintaining its aspect ratio. The image will cover the entire container, but part of the image might be clipped (cropped) if its aspect ratio does not match the container's aspect ratio.

object-fit: contain : Behavior: The image is resized to fit entirely within the container while maintaining its aspect ratio. The entire image will be visible, but there may be empty space (padding) around the image if the container has a different aspect ratio than the image.

object-fit: fill : Behavior: The image is resized to fill the entire container, but it will stretch to match both the width and height of the container, regardless of its aspect ratio. This can distort the image if the aspect ratio of the container is different from the image.
